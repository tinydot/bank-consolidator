🤖 Ask AI about your money

Ask things like "can I afford a $400 desktop speaker?" or "what's my coffee habit costing me each month?". Claude reads your local database (read-only) to answer.

Set up — add your Anthropic API key
⚠️ Your data leaves the device for this feature. To answer questions, the app sends relevant rows from your local database to Anthropic's API. The rest of the app stays fully offline. Your key is stored only in this browser and sent only to api.anthropic.com.

Get one from the Anthropic Console. Stored only in this browser (localStorage).

Personalise — tell Claude about your situation

Free-text context added to every question so answers fit your life — where you live, what counts as normal spending, your goals. Stored only in this browser (localStorage); leave blank for generic advice. Amend it any time as your situation changes.

Read-only: the AI can look at your data but can never change it. Aggregate lookups (totals/counts) are sent automatically; anything row-level (individual transactions) asks you to approve first. Your questions and the answers are saved with your database, so they persist across reloads and are included in your local .db export and Google Drive backup — but the fetched rows behind each answer are not stored. Use Delete history to wipe the conversation.
